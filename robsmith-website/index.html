Meet Rob Smith III

With over 30 years in real estate, I've built my career on one simple principle: putting my clients first. Originally licensed in Georgia in 1999, I earned my Broker's License in 2004 and became a Lifetime Member of the Million Dollar Council for surpassing $5 million in sales. Most recently, I was recognized as a Masters Ruby Producer in 2024.

I moved to Virginia in 2010 and quickly fell in love with the land and the people. Since then, I've served as Managing Broker, Property Manager, Sales Manager, and Team Leader across Central and Northern Virginia, as well as Washington, D.C. During the 2008-2010 foreclosure crisis, I served as a preferred broker for major lenders including Countrywide, Bank of America, and JP Morgan Chase, successfully closing over 300 REO properties.

My understanding of architecture and interior design helps buyers see a property's true potential - often identifying opportunities others miss. When I'm not helping clients, you'll find me at my family's vineyard and brewery in Spotsylvania with my Jack Russell, Bailey. A portion of every commission goes to Children's Miracle Network on behalf of the Miss Virginia Scholarship Organization.
