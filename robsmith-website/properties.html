French Country Elegance on 101 Acres

Experience the timeless charm of French Country living blended with refined European elegance at Stronghorus Estate. This magnificent gated property offers over 101 meticulously fenced acres - a private haven for equestrian enthusiasts and those seeking a luxurious rural lifestyle.

Originally a modest two-room farmhouse dating back to the late 1800s, this estate has been thoughtfully expanded and transformed over the past 130+ years into the stunning residence you see today. Currently undergoing a comprehensive $300,000 renovation, updates span the primary residence, guest house, pool, detached garage, and 6-stall stable.

The property includes a charming 1 bedroom/1 bath guest house, a sparkling 20x50 inground pool, 2-car detached garage, and professional equestrian facilities. With its rich history and modern amenities, Stronghorus Estate represents a rare opportunity to own a piece of Virginia's heritage.
